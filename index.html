Who Am I?

From a humble sales executive to becoming a director of retail, training & projects across 24 countries in the Middle East & Africa. I have over 15 years of experience in Retail, Wholesale & Footwear specializing in Trade Marketing, Store Planning, Project Management & Visual Merchandising across 27 countries in the Middle East and Africa region. In 2021, I founded start up Tech Company in the based in the Philippines with the aim to make life better through technology for the Filipinos by Filipinos.
